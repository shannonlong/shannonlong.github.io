Hi!,

I’m Shannon Long.

I’m an illustrator/animator from Halifax, Nova Scotia.

Born and raised in NS, I found my inspiration for creation in cartoons, comics, anime, video games, graffiti, roller skate culture and all things cute and colourful.

I currently work as a Digital Game Artist, focusing on graphics and animations for casual gaming. I also work professionally as an illustrator in traditional mediums.

My current favourite works being acrylic paint on found objects, to give the abandoned new purpose
