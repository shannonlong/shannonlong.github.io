Shannon is a playful artist who creates colorful and quirky pieces inspired by the worlds of nerd, pop, and queer culture.

Her vibrant artwork celebrates the joy of loving who and what you love unapologetically, blending inclusivity and individuality with fun cartoony imagery.

Based in Halifax, Nova Scotia, she uses her art to foster connections within the community, spark smiles and brighten days.
